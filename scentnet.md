# ScentNet

ScentNet is a large open multimodal dataset for the advancement of olfaction in AI and robotics.

Detailed technical specification is in development and expected 1 February 2026.
Data releases will be made available [here on HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/kordelfrance/scentnet-dataset).

ScentNet data is acquired through the Scentience Sigma app. Sigma is available for free on the Apple App Store [here](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/scentience-sigma/id6756134989).
