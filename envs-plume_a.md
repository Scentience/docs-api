# Scentience Environments: Plume Model

The Scentience plume environment creates a sandbox for training robots on tasks such as scent-based navigation and plume tracking.

All environments are standardized on the Gym/Gymnasium environment protocol for easy drop-in.

You can access the Scentience Plume Environments [here.](https://github.com/Scentience/scentience-plume-envs)
